Last updated: June 7, 2026
This is a template that describes how the AROBOTIX website behaves today: an informational site with no accounts, no forms, no payments, and no analytics. It must be revised by qualified counsel and updated the moment the Site begins collecting data through forms, logins, cookies, or analytics tools. It is not legal advice.

This Privacy Policy explains how AROBOTIX Corp., a Delaware corporation with operations in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam ("AROBOTIX," "we," "us"), handles information in connection with the website at www.arobotix.com (the "Site").

01The short version

The Site is a brochure-style informational website. We do not ask you to create an account, fill in forms, or make payments, and we do not run advertising or analytics trackers. We do not set our own cookies. The main way we receive your information is if you choose to email us. The Site also loads a small number of third-party resources that, by the nature of how the web works, receive your IP address (see Section 4).

02Information you give us directly

If you contact us, for example by emailing info@arobotix.com, we receive the information you choose to include, such as your name, email address, and the contents of your message. We use it only to respond to you and to manage our relationship with you.

03Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically log standard technical information when the Site is served, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, referring page, and time of access. This is used for security, troubleshooting, and basic operation. We do not use this information to build profiles of visitors.

08Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described here or as required by law, then delete or anonymize it.

The Site is intended for a professional, business audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
